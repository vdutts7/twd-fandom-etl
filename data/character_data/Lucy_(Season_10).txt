Lucy
 is a survivor of the outbreak in 
AMC
's 
The Walking Dead
. She is a resident of 
Bloodsworth Island
 who was imprisoned by 
Virgil
 following his massacre of the other islanders. After being freed by 
Michonne Grimes
, she departs from the island with the other 
former
 
prisoners
 and begins a new journey elsewhere.

Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Season 10
[
]


"
What We Become
"
[
]


Lucy is discovered in a jail cell alongside Jeremiah and Celeste by Michonne shortly after she is captured by Virgil. However, she and her friends are freed after Michonne gains the upperhand on Virgil and escapes from the jail. While the survivors capture Virgil, they spare his life and allow him to remain on the island as they take a boat and leave the island.
