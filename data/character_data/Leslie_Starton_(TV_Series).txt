Pre-Apocalypse
[
]


Virginia
[
]


Nothing is known about Leslie's life prior to or as the outbreak began, except that he resided in a 
houseboat
. He was also a gun enthusiast who collected several antique handguns and rifles.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Virginia
[
]


Leslie was living in a houseboat in a lake not far away from the 
Alexandria Safe Zone
. At some point, Leslie's home was attacked by another survivor or group of survivors. Leslie was killed during the shootout, but was apparently able to fend off the attacker(s), as his supplies weren't looted.



Season 7
[
]


"
Hearts Still Beating
"
[
]


Rick
 and 
Aaron
 come across his boathouse while looking for supplies. They come across his message to any survivors wishing to steal from him, however, both of them wearily disregard the message. They see that the lake where the boat is at is surrounded by walkers but they manage to go across the lake and get onto the boat where a dead Leslie is on. They take his supplies and leave his corpse on the boat.
