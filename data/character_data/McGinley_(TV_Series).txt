Atlanta, Georgia
[
]


Nothing is known about McGinley's life prior to or as the outbreak began, except that he was an officer for the 
Atlanta Police Department
.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Season 5
[
]


"
Coda
"
[
]


Officer McGinley and his partner Officer 
Franco
 find survivor 
Rick Grimes
 on the top level of a parking garage the hospital uses to keep vehicles. Rick explains that they have some of their colleagues hostage and would like to exchange them for 
Beth Greene
 and 
Carol Peletier
, who have been taken by the hospital. McGinley informs Lieutenant 
Dawn Lerner
 of the situation and brings Rick's group and the officers to the hospital. He joins his fellow officers during the hostage exchange and witnesses the deaths of Dawn and Beth. He remains behind at the hospital with Dr. 
Steven Edwards
, the patients, and the other officers.
