Overview
[
]


Though only seen briefly, George seemed to be an outspoken, confident, and smart individual. He seemed to be a loyal 
Savior
 and was on good terms with his fellow allies. While waiting for a herd to pass, George comments on how 
Fat Joey
 was good for setting up rigged explosives for dealing with a herd. He even defends Fat Joey when 
Chris
 makes a demeaning joke about him, despite Joey usually being getting little respect amongst the Saviors. He also seems to show some knowledge about military tactics. However, George ends up being quickly shot to death after shortly after spotting 
Carl Grimes
.



Pre-Apocalypse
[
]


Location Unknown
[
]


Nothing is known about George's life prior to or as the outbreak began.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Washington, D.C.
[
]


At some point in the apocalypse, George somehow came into contact with a group named the "Saviors" which he subsequently joined. 

George aided his group in terrorizing other survivors and communities around the area into subjugating to Negan's will, where their victims would be forced to give half their supplies in exchange for not being murdered.

Fat Joey
 was useful in finding the explosives while scavenging. Upon arriving at the Sanctuary, George is suddenly shot and killed after discovering Carl hiding in the truck.
