Trivia
[
]


In the episode "
Go Getters
", Stephanie was originally going to have a line and be credited for her appearance. However, her line was cut and she remained uncredited.


The character is named after her actress, Stephanie Rae.


References
[
]






↑
 
Stephanie has not been seen since Season 7 and several events have occurred that left her fate unclear. However, with the Saviors' tainted weapons attack on the Hilltop, several people are killed as indicated by dialogue in the episode and the large amount of additional graves shown in the graveyard. Because of this, and Stephanie's continued absence, it is likely she is among the casualties of All Out War, if she did not die sooner.
