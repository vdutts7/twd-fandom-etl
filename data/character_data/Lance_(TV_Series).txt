Location Unknown

Nothing is known about Lance's life prior to or as the outbreak began.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Washington, D.C.
[
]


At some point in the apocalypse, Lance somehow came into contact with a group named "the Saviors" which he subsequently joined.

Lance aided his group in terrorizing other survivors and communities around the area into subjugating to Negan's will, where their victims would be forced to give half their supplies in exchange for not being murdered.



Season 8
[
]


"
Wrath
"
[
]


Lance is at the 
Sanctuary
 with the other Saviors getting ready for the final assault when he and a group of Saviors are dispatched by Negan to set up a roadblock, when in reality they are being used as a decoy. At the roadblock, Lance and his men are attacked by 
Rick
 and the Militia and all are killed.



Death
[
]


Killed By


Negan Smith
 
(Caused)


Negan deliberately sends Lance along with a large group of Saviors to set up a roadblock and walkers on a road and be sacrificed as part of his plan to lead Rick and the Militia into a trap.



The Militia


Lance is shot to death with his fellow Saviors after the Militia attacks them on the roadside.
