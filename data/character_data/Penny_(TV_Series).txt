Season 10
[
]


"
Morning Star
"
[
]


Penny and 
Felix
 are instructed by 
Yumiko Okumura
 to scout the nearby area and radio back as soon as they spot the Whisperers. Unfortunately while scouting, the pair are captured and hanged by the Whisperers. Their reanimated corpses are discovered by the caravan Daryl is leading at a roadblock created by 
Negan Smith
.



Death
[
]


Killed By


The Whisperers
 
(Alive)


Daryl Dixon
, 
Lydia
, or 
Dianne
 
(Zombified)


After being hanged by the Whisperers while out on patrol, a zombified Penny is found by the caravan of Hilltoppers heading towards Oceanside. Penny is then put down by either Daryl, Lydia, or Dianne.
