Overview
[
]


Derek is a rough, aggressive, and crude man. He is shown to be a loyal 
Savior
 and has no mercy towards his enemies. Even to his own allies, he often speaks to them in a really hostile and aggressive manner when they mess up, such as berating a Savior for shooting 
Dr. Harlan Carson
. He later takes part in the Savior's attack on the 
Hilltop
 and is relentless in the fight. He manages to cut 
Tobin
 though he is quickly killed after. Despite his death, the damage is already done and Tobin ends up turning due to Derek's weapon being tainted with walker blood, causing the deaths of many residents.



Pre-Apocalypse
[
]


Location Unknown
[
]


Nothing is known about Derek's life prior to or as the outbreak began.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Washington, D.C.
[
]


At some point in the apocalypse, Derek somehow came into contact with a group named "the Saviors" which he subsequently joined.

Derek aided his group in terrorizing other survivors and communities around the area into subjugating to Negan's will, where their victims would be forced to give half their supplies in exchange for not being murdered.



Season 8
[
]


"
Dead or Alive Or
"
[
]


While searching for the missing Alexandrians, Derek and his fellow Saviors get a call on the radio that 
Gabriel
 and 
Dr. Carson
 have gone missing from the Sanctuary along with 
Jeremy
's green Jaguar. They quickly get in their truck and drive away in search of the two missing prisoners. Later that day, the group finds the escapees in a garage and takes them into custody as Derek berates a fellow Savior for shooting Dr. Carson. When they arrive at the 
bullet factory outpost
, Derek and 
Mel
 drop off Gabriel at the outpost as Eugene's newest worker.



"
Do Not Send Us Astray
"
[
]


Derek is among the Saviors to attack the Hilltop and slashes 
Tobin
 across the chest. Before he can kill Tobin, Derek is shot and killed by 
Carol
.



Death
[
]


Killed By


Carol Peletier


During the Saviors' attack on the Hilltop, Derek attacks Tobin with his tainted knife, seriously wounding him. Before he can kill him, Derek is shot by Carol.
