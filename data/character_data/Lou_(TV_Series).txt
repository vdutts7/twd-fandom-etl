Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Season 4
[
]


"
Claimed
"
[
]


Lou is first seen inside the house that 
Rick Grimes
, his son 
Carl
, and 
Michonne Hawthorne
 have been staying in with the group he is with. He is encountered by Rick as he tries to find a way out of the house without being seen. As Rick has a close call, he hides in the bathroom while Lou is sitting on the toilet seat. Rick hits Lou in the stomach with a trophy and then struggles with him as he begins to choke him. Lou tries to reach for a pair of scissors that are lying on a countertop to defend himself, but he ultimately ends up dying. Shortly after, Lou reanimates as a zombie and can be heard attacking the other members of his group who are in the house.
