Pre-Apocalypse


Location Unknown


Nothing is known about Rowan's life prior to or as the outbreak began.



Post-Apocalypse


Woodbury, Georgia


Rowan joined the Woodbury settlement at one point and lavished in the return to normalcy that was given to her and others. She grew increasingly close to 
the Governor
 until she eventually became his lover (albeit presumably in secret).



Season 3


"
Walk With Me
"


The morning after 
Andrea
 and 
Michonne
 arrive in Woodbury, they are given a tour around town by Rowan. As they walk up the main street, she talks about how there are 73 people living in Woodbury, and the 74th is on its way, referencing to 
Eileen
 who is heavily pregnant and walks by saying hello to Rowan. Later that afternoon, she is among the townspeople to crowd around the newly-acquired military vehicles brought in by the 
Governor's
 men and listens to the leader's speech. That night, Rowan is asleep naked in the Governor's bed.



"
Welcome to the Tombs
"


Following the failed 
prison assault
, Rowan's fate is unclear. However, she is most likely dead as it is likely she was among the Woodbury soldiers killed during the prison raid or massacred by the Governor out on the road as she was not seen with the remaining Woodburians arriving at the prison.
[1]




Death


Killed By


Philip Blake
 
(Caused or Direct)


Rowan is among the residents shot to death by the Governor after refusing to return to the Prison to continue fighting.
