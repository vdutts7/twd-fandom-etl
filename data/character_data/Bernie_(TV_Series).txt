Nothing is known about Bernie's life prior to or as the outbreak began.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


At some point in the apocalypse, Bernie encountered Magna's group and joined them.



Season 9
[
]


"
Stradivarius
"
[
]


A massive 
herd
 attacks 
Magna's group
. Later, 
Michonne
 realizes that it's the same herd that destroyed Magna's rig when she spots Bernie amongst them, ripped apart and wearing the same shirt he had on in the story the group had earlier told the Alexandrians.

As an undead Bernie approaches his former friends, who are stunned and saddened to see him, Michonne mercifully drives the tip of her 
katana
 through the back of his head, putting him down.



Death
[
]


Killed By


Zombies
 
(Alive)


As Bernie and the rest of Magna's group flee their camp, he is unable to escape in time and is devoured by walkers.



Michonne Grimes
 
(Zombified)


After being discovered by Michonne in the herd that killed him, Michonne mercifully stabs Bernie in the back of his head, putting him down.
