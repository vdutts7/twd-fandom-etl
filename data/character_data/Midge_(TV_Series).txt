Pre-Apocalypse
[
]


Location Unknown
[
]


Nothing is known about Midge's life prior to or as the outbreak began, except that she had a twin sister named 
Hilda
.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Virginia
[
]


Midge and Hilda survived the early stages of the outbreak and eventually they found themselves as members of a large group of survivors led by a woman named 
Georgie
. Eventually, they became trusted confidants of their leader and they would serve as her escort during her mission of seeking communities to negotiate with them.



Season 8
[
]


"
The Key
"
[
]


Midge and Hilda stand outside their van in the middle of the road, waiting for the 
Hilltoppers
 to respond to their offer of trading phonograph records for food and knowledge. When 
Maggie
 and her companions arrive at the meeting point, Georgie exits the vehicle and introduces herself. However, Midge and Hilda are stripped of their weapons under Maggie's order and the group is taken to the Hilltop as prisoners until further notice. Later that day, Maggie finally allows them to leave before reaching an agreement with Georgie on improving their community through Georgie's knowledge gained for her next visit in the future. After some parting words by Georgie, Midge and the others drive off.

[
]


"
The Bridge
"
[
]


Though not mentioned it's implied that she and Hilda are alive due to 
Jesus
 mentioning Georgie's invitation to the group after the time skip.



"
Stradivarius
"
[
]


When talking to 
Tara
, Jesus mentions that Midge and Hilda dropped off another one of Maggie's letters that morning.



Season 10
[
]


"
Home Sweet Home
"
[
]


Maggie reveals that a couple of years before her return, Georgie left with the twins to go help another city while Maggie stayed with the community they had been working on. Shortly thereafter, the community where Maggie was fell and she hasn't seen Midge since. As a result, Maggie doesn't know if she's still alive or not.
