Overview
[
]


Alves is a greedy and uncaring man. He is loyal to 
Sebastian
 for an easier life and has no problems with him sending 30-40 people to their deaths for money. His lack of care for other lives goes so far as to not caring about children, threatening to make 
Daryl
 and 
Rosita
's kids orphans. This greed leads to his death at the hands of 
Mercer
.

Season 11
[
]


"
The Rotten Core
"
[
]


Alves and Castle pull Rosita and Daryl away from their assignment to investigate a 
herd
 of walkers on the north perimeter. Alves and Castle take Daryl and Rosita to the northern perimeter. Sebastian joins them and orders Daryl and Rosita to retrieve a stash of cash hidden in a 
house
 on the other side of the herd. Daryl's group meets up with Alves and Castle and learns that Sebastian has already sent 30 or 40 people to look for the cash. Mercer shoots Alves and Castle dead.
