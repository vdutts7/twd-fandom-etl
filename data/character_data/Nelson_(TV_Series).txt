Pre-Apocalypse

Location Unknown
[
]


Nothing is known about Nelson's life prior to or as the outbreak began.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Washington, D.C.
[
]


At some point in the apocalypse, Nelson somehow came into contact with a group named "the Saviors" which he subsequently joined.

Nelson aided his group in terrorizing other survivors and communities around the area into subjugating to Negan's will, where their victims would be forced to give half their supplies in exchange for not being murdered.

Carol
 and the Kingdommers. He throws a smoke grenade and manages to flee as walkers close in on the survivors.

Shiva

Death
[
]


Killed By


Ezekiel Sutton
 
(Caused)


Shiva


After escaping a group of Kingdom soldiers following the assault on the Sanctuary, Nelson attempts to flee to one of the Saviors outposts but just as the Kingdom soldiers catch up to him, he is mauled to death by Shiva, who was released to hunt him down on Ezekiel's orders.
