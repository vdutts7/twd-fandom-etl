Almost nothing is known about Franklin's life prior to or as the outbreak began, except that he served in the National Guard. 



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Season 3
[
]


"
Walk With Me
"
[
]


Franklin was a passenger in a 
helicopter
 along with 
Welles
 and 
Sean
. After being caught in wind turbulence which caused a malfunction, the helicopter erupted in a small explosion and lost control. As the helicopter lost control, Franklin hit his head inside the helicopter. He was stuck in the helicopter as it crashed into the woods and was killed by the impact of the crash.



Death
[
]


Killed By


Welles
 
(Alive, Accidental)


Helicopter Crash
 
(Alive)


The helicopter that Franklin is riding in suffers mechanical problems, causing it to crash in the woods, cutting through the trees.

Franklin dies from the impact of the crash, possibly due to internal injuries.



Philip Blake
 
(Zombified)


Franklin reanimates off screen and is put down by The Governor.
