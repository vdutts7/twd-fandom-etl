Trivia
[
]


Maya is credited as 
Woman
 in "
Forget
".

This character's name is revealed in "The Next World" after her name is added to the memorial wall as a result of the herd that attacked Alexandria, killing several people.
[1]


Maya is a TV Series adaptation of 
Barbara
 from the Comic Series; in the Comic Series, at the welcome party, Michonne snaps at Barbara after being overwhelmed by Barbara's questions.

Barbara in the TV Series is an homage to the character of Barbara from 
Night of the Living Dead
.


References
[
]






↑
 
According to a set designer from Season 6, the name "Maya" refers to Maia Moss-Fife's character from the previous season. Similar to how the names of extras killed off in "JSS" were written on the wall, Maia's name was added to the wall as a casualty of the herd attack in "No Way Out". Unfortunately, one of the crew members was not aware of how Maia's name is spelled, and painted the name as "Maya" as it is more commonly known as.
