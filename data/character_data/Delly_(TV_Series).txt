Season 6
[

"
Always Accountable
"
[
]


Delly and Carla are found covered in glass, melted from the burned out greenhouse in the forest they died in. Unbeknownst to Tina, she and Carla had reanimated into zombies and were able to attack her, breaking the glass layer on top of them, when she got too close, resulting in her death. One moment later she is put down by Daryl.
