Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Washington, D.C.
[
]


At some point in the apocalypse, Dino somehow came into contact with a group named "the Saviors" which he subsequently joined. 

Dino aided his group in terrorizing other survivors and communities around the area into subjugating to Negan's will, where their victims would be forced to give half their supplies in exchange for not being murdered.



Season 8
[
]


"
The Damned
"
[
]


Dino is stationed at 
Regina
's outpost and serves under the command of 
Mara
. He is at the café when the Alexandrians pull up in the armored vehicles and open fire. As Mara fights back, she hides behind a car with Dino and informs the nearby Saviors that they must push back. Eventually, Dino is gunned down and killed and shortly after, reanimates. He proceeds to attack and kill Mara just as she realizes the Alexandrians' true plan.
