Pre-Apocalypse
[
]


Location Unknown
[
]


Nothing is known about Noah's life prior to or as the outbreak began, except that he lived with his mother at the very least, and had a 'BB gun'/airsoft rifle before his mother confiscated the toy.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Season 3
[
]


"
I Ain't a Judas
"
[
]


Andrea storms out of 
the Governor
's apartment to find 
Karen
 arguing with 
Martinez
, saying that Noah has asthma and shouldn't fight. Later, when the Governor evaluates all of the able-bodied townspeople to be trained in firearms, 
Andrea
 tells the Governor that he has asthma, in which the Governor asks Noah if he has ever fired a gun. Noah replies that he once had a BB gun, but his mother took it away. The Governor pats him on the shoulder, amused, and reassures him that Martinez will be able to train him properly with more advanced weaponry for the upcoming war. When Andrea returns at night in the police car, Noah, on wall duty, is ordered by Martinez to duck.



"
Welcome to the Tombs
"
[
]


Following the failed prison assault, Noah was among the townspeople murdered by the Governor out on the road after they refused to return to the prison.
[1]

Trivia
[
]


Noah's actor, 
Parker Wierling
, was at the Walkers Stalkers event and confirmed he was not Karen's son. In addition to this, during a MTV interview with 
Laurie Holden
 and 
Melissa Ponzio
, they also confirmed that Noah and Karen are not related. Instead, Laurie and Melissa speculate on Karen's backstory, suggesting she was a schoolteacher and that is why she cared for Noah.
[2]


Noah is one of five characters known to have asthma.

The other four are 
Kara
, 
Mr. Gilbert
, 
Warren Bedford
, and 
Tess' Son
.
