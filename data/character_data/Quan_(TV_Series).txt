Morning Star
"
[
]


Quan is among the Hilltoppers going about their business in town, though on high alert. After the caravan of children return to town, he listens as 
Daryl
, 
Dianne
, and the others discuss that the roads leading away from Hilltop have been blocked and that the Whisperers are closing in on them. He then helps the other townspeople prepare for the incoming battle and stays within the walls with the other residents for safety.



"
Walk With Us
"
[
]


Quan is among the residents to help defend the Hilltop as the Whisperers and their horde push into the settlement. He helps load the catapults with stones as they are launched onto the horde outside the walls. Ultimately, it can be assumed that Quan was killed by the Whisperers or the zombies that continued to push into the burning Hilltop, as he was not seen among the several survivor groups to escape Hilltop, and was never seen at any point afterwards again.
