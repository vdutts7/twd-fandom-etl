Overview
[
]


Nabila is a kind and sweet woman. Though initially just a gardener, she eventually goes on to become a soldier for the 
Militia
. She is a loving wife to her husband 
Jerry
 and caring mother to her children, usually seen taking care of them.

Nabila is shown to be extremely brave and willing to risk her life for what she believes in. Examples of this are her joining the Militia and Ezekiel's rebellion against the sadistic 
Warden
 in 
Alexandria
, bravely standing in front of a firing squad to protect 
Negan
 and 
Annie
.



Pre-Apocalypse
[
]


Location Unknown
[
]


Nothing is known about Nabila's life prior to or as the outbreak began. She was likely a follower of Islam before the start of the apocalypse, as it's unlikely she became devout after it commenced.

]


Washington, D.C.

At some point in the apocalypse, Nabila joined a community called 
the Kingdom
 where she became a gardener.

Nabila approaches

Nabila then informs Ezekiel that the Kingdom‚Äôs gardens are infested with weevils and the crops in the garden are therefore ruined. Nabila then flees the Kingdom with the rest of the group to the safe house.

When a group of walkers suddenly ambushed the lumber yard, Nabila is among those to arrive in time to save

Six years later, Nabila spends time with Jerry and her children in the Kingdom as everyone is setting things ready for the fair. She then watches as Jerry leaves on a deer hunting mission. At night, Nabila sleeps as Jerry hold their daughter from her crib.

the Highwaymen

Later that day, Nabila welcomes the first half of the Hilltop delegation and their escorts to the Kingdom. She chats with 
Earl
 and 
Tammy Rose
 about the abandoned 
Whisperer baby
 when Tammy remarks that she cares for the baby and would like to care for it, to which Nabila smiles delightfully at the prospect.

 Ezekiel says as everyone celebrates and applauds.

Frankie

Siddiq

"
The Storm
"
[
]


Several months after the fair massacre, the Kingdommers are forced to abandon their fallen community due to an incoming blizzard and move to the Hilltop. When snow starts falling later that day, Nabila and the convoy is forced to take shelter inside the Sanctuary for the night where she helps her family rest and stay warm.  

Later that night, the convoy leaves the factory and is forced to cross into 
Alpha
's territory. When they encounter a frozen lake, members of the group kill walkers that emerge from the snowbanks before the whole convoy manages to escape safely across the ice. Nabila embraces her family, glad for their safety. The next morning, Nabila and the convoy finally arrive at the Hilltop where she spends time with her family in their new community.

[
]


"
Silence the Whisperers
"
[
]


Nabila is alerted to the commotion of a tree falling onto a house and a portion of Hilltop's walls. She then helps the other residents rescue the trapped injured from the rubble. The next day, Nabila and the other residents kill the incoming walkers with the help of the Alexandria convoy when the herd invades the community that night. 



"
Bonds
"
[
]


Nabila carries a box of radio equipment to the attic of the Barrington House at Hilltop. She helps 
Eugene
 set up a new relay station at Hilltop before returning to care for her children downstairs.



"
Morning Star
"
[
]


Nabila attends an emergency meeting in the Barrington House with the leaders of Hilltop and Alexandria and other concerned residents. She listens as the other discuss what to do in the face of the impending Whisperer threat. After it is decided that they evacuate the children and make a stand at Hilltop, she joins the caravan with her three children only to be brought back to Hilltop after finding a roadblock and the zombified bodies of Felix and Penny hanging from a tree. At Hilltop, she remains inside the Barrington House with the other children and parents and witnesses Alden refusing to allow Mary to see her nephew. When the Whisperers and their horde attack the Hilltop at night, she remains inside the Barrington House for shelter.



"
Walk With Us
"
[
]


Nabila is among a group of survivors consisting of Rosita, Daryl, Dianne, Jerry, Mariam, two Hilltop women, and two Alexandrian soldiers trudging through the wilderness as they attempt to reach the rendezvous point to find Ezekiel, Earl, and the children, including her son Ezra and daughter Aliyah. Having been injured with burns from the Hilltop battle, Nabila struggles on the journey to the rendezvous point, expressing her grief as she yearns to be reunited with her children. Upon reaching a rendezvous point with no signs of the children, she begins to break down and panic as Jerry attempts to calm her down. She stays behind with Mariam and the women as Ezekiel, Jerry, Daryl, and the others search the area for the children.



"
Look at the Flowers
"
[
]


Nabila meets up with the others at the rendezvous point. She and the other Hilltop survivors welcome Magna back and later listens to Eugene as he explains that he has been talking to a woman over the radio and plans to meet her as he believes she can help them.



"
A Certain Doom
"
[
]


Nabila is among the Hilltop survivors to relocate to an abandoned hospital referred to as the "
Tower
" where she and her family, and dozens of others, seek refuge on the upper floors of the hospital. As a group of survivors enact a plan to lure the herd away, others defend the hospital from the Whisperers and snipe as many as they can from the windows. When the Whisperers eventually break into the hospital, Nabila and the others are evacuated safely from the hospital via the elevator shafts and basement tunnels. After 
Beta
 and the Whisperers are wiped out, the remaining survivors congregate in the woods where the injured are treated and families are reunited. Nabila sits with her children as 
Bertie
 greets them and chats with the family.



"
Home Sweet Home
"
[
]


A short time after making it to the rendezvous in the woods with the other survivors, Nabila accompanies Barbara and Lydia to Alexandria, as she is among the refugees assigned to live there after Hilltop was destroyed by the Whisperers. After running into a minor obstacle on the road, they are helped by Negan and resume their journey as Maggie and Judith emerge from the woods. A few days later, Nabila helps clean up the destruction in Alexandria left behind by the Whisperers. She works alongside Barbara, Carol, Negan, Lydia, and several other residents when Daryl returns with Kelly and Maggie‚Äôs group. She then watches as Maggie, her son Hershel, and the other members of her group enter Alexandria through the fallen wall and begin to explore the streets of their new home.

Nabila and Barbara grab the babies 
Mariam
 and

Nabila and her family are among the Hilltop refugees to move from Alexandria to the Commonwealth after accepting Lance's offer. A month later, she and her family are enjoying the Halloween festivities outside of 
Union Station
 in the city. She watches on as her husband plays with their kids at the petting zoo with Ezekiel while holding a sleeping Mariam. When Ezekiel gives Shiva's collar and chain to Ezra, she smiles on at the generosity of their family friend.



"
A New Deal
"
[
]


Nabila and her three children attend the Founders' Day celebrations in Union Square, with her two eldest children playing in the petting zoo. Later, she and her family watch the wrestling match with Ezekiel and Carol until a zombie outbreak caused mass panic in the square. Holding Mariam in her arms, she and her children follow Ezekiel through the crowd to safety.

 by the windmill, standing next to
