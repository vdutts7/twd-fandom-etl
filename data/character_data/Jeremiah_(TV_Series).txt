"
What We Become
"
[
]


Jeremiah is discovered in a jail cell alongside Lucy and Celeste by Michonne shortly after she is captured by Virgil. However, he and his friends are freed after Michonne gains the upper hand on Virgil and escapes from the jail. While the survivors capture Virgil, they spare his life and allow him to remain on the island as they take a boat and leave the island.
