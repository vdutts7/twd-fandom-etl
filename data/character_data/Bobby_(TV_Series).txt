Pre-Apocalypse
[
]


Location Unknown
[
]


Nothing is known about Bobby's life prior to or as the outbreak began.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Virginia
[
]


At some point in the apocalypse, Bobby joined a community called the 
Alexandria Safe Zone
 where, upon being interviewed by 
Deanna
, he was appointed as one of the guards at the gates.



Season 5
[
]


"
Remember
"
[
]


Bobby is on duty at the front gate and allows 
Aiden
 and his party to return from their supply run. He watches in horror with the other townspeople as Aiden and newcomer 
Glenn
 start fighting in the street.



"
Spend
"
[
]


Bobby is working at the Woodlands Mall construction site gathering materials with the construction crew when the site is attacked by a herd. He helps fight off the walkers after watching as Abraham rescues an injured 
Francine
, who was abandoned by 
Tobin
. Moments later, Bobby remains working after Abraham starts leading the team.



"
Conquer
"
[
]


Bobby is among the townspeople present at the community forum to discuss 
Rick
's fate. He also witnesses the murder of 
Reg
, which he is horrified at, and the subsequent execution of 
Pete
.



Season 6
[
]


"
First Time Again
"
[
]


Bobby attends the meeting at Deanna's house regarding how to handle the mega herd at the quarry. In the following days, he is among the volunteers to help build the wall on one of the roads to deter the mega herd in the operation to lure it away from Alexandria.



"
JSS
"
[
]


Bobby is killed when 
the Wolves
 invade Alexandria and start ransacking houses and murdering residents.



"
Now
"
[
]


The next day, Bobby's body is gathered by 
Tobin
 and 
Bruce
 and buried along with the other slain Alexandrians. His name is then added to the memorial wall by 
Betsy
 and two other 
women
.



Death
[
]


Killed By




Aaron
 
(Indirectly Caused)


Aaron leaves his backpack at Del Arno Foods, which causes the Wolves to locate and attack Alexandria.



The Wolves


As the Wolves make their way into Alexandria, Bobby is one of the many residents to be killed.
