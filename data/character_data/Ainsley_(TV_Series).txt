Post-Apocalypse

Season 10
[
]


"
Home Sweet Home
"
[
]


Ainsley and 
Gus
, two members of 
Maggie
's group, are introduced to 
Daryl
, who briefly held 
Maya
 hostage. Maya explains that the place they were staying in was set on fire, and that 
Jen
 and 
Billy
 didn't make it out, while 
Matty
 was killed by something in the woods. She didn't see who did it, but Gus is certain the Reapers are responsible. Maggie asks about 
Hershel
, to which Ainsley says that he was found by 
Kim
, but she hasn't seen either one since. The conversation is cut short when Gus is abruptly shot in the throat with a silenced weapon.

The group runs for cover as the 
unknown assailant
 takes pot shots at them. As Daryl and Maggie sneak through the woods, Ainsley has been shot in the stomach. Unfortunately, Daryl and Maggie can't help her, as they both agree that it's a trap and she dies of her wounds.



Death
[
]


Killed By


Pope
 
(Caused)


The Attacker


Pope sends a Reaper out to hunt down Maggie's group. After the Attacker arrives in the woods, he discovers their location and shoots Ainsley through the stomach. Given her brain was not destroyed, she may have reanimated later.
