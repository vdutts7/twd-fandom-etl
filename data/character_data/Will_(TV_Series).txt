Pre-Apocalypse
[

Location Unknown
[
]


Nothing is known about Will's life prior to or as the outbreak began.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Virginia
[
]


At some point in the apocalypse, Will joined a community called the 
Alexandria Safe Zone
 where, upon being interviewed by 
Deanna
, he was appointed as a supply runner in a team alongside 
Aiden
, 
Nicholas
, and three other residents where they searched for supplies around the town.

During a supply run in an abandoned town, the scouting party was attacked by walkers while Will was left behind by Aiden and Nicholas out of cowardice. Over time, a partially devoured Will reanimated and, due to being pinned and immobilized under a car, gradually deteriorated.



Season 6
[
]


"
Thank You
"
[
]


When 
Glenn
 and 
Nicholas
 head out into the abandoned town to set fire to a feed store to lure walkers away, the two come across the zombified Will still pinned under a car. Feeling guilty and responsible for having caused his death, Nicholas stabs him in the head to put him down.



Death
[
]


Killed By


Aiden Monroe
 
(Caused, Alive)


Zombies
 
(Alive)


Nicholas
 
(Caused, Alive; Zombified)


While Will, Aiden, and Nicholas were on a supply run, they ran into trouble with some walkers. As a result, Aiden and Nicholas chose to leave Will behind, resulting in his death.

Sometime later, he is found reanimated and is stabbed in the head out of mercy by Nicholas.
