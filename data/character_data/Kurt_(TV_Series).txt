Season 8
[
]


"
Do Not Send Us Astray
"
[
]


Following 
the Saviors
' attack on 
Hilltop
, Kurt tends to the wounded alongside 
Siddiq
 and 
Dana
. That night, he is on nurse duty as he watches over Tobin and 
Kevin
, both recovering from their injuries from the attack. He is awakened by Kevin's groans in the middle of the night at 3:26 am and decides to go find Dana. Before Kurt can exit the trailer, he is attacked from behind by a reanimated 
Tobin
 and promptly killed. A short time later, a reanimated Kurt is along the zombified residents and refugees to attack the people sleeping inside the Barrington House. During the attack, Kurt is put down by 
Michonne
's katana as he is held against the wall by 
Jesus
. The next morning, he is buried in the graveyard with the other casualties.
