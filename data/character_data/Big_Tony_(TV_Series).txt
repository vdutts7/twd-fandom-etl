Pre-Apocalypse
[
]


Barnesville, Georgia
[
]


Almost nothing is known about Big Tony's life prior to or as the outbreak began, except that he ran a gas station and auto repair shop in or near Barnesville, Georgia with his family.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


At some point after the outbreak began, Tony and his employees entered into a 
suicide
 pact. While Tony hung himself from the celling fan, the other three drank antifreeze. All four reanimated and were left locked in the store together. Eventually, Tony's weight was too much for the fan and he fell to the ground and became trapped beneath a collapsed fan and other debris behind the counter inside the auto repair shop.



Season 4
[
]


"
Indifference
"
[
]


Bob
 and 
Daryl
 find Big Tony buried inside debris in the auto shop. Bob feels bad for the trapped walker and stabs him in the head with a screwdriver that was used as a keychain, putting him down.
