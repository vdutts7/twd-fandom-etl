Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Washington, D.C.
[
]


At some point in the apocalypse, Huck somehow came into contact with a group called "the Saviors" which she subsequently joined upon where she was appointed as one of the Sanctuary guards.  

Huck aided her group in terrorizing other survivors and communities around the area into subjugating to Negan's will, where their victims would be forced to give half their supplies in exchange for not being murdered.

Season 8

"

[

Huck is working at the 
Shephard Office Plaza

Mara

Todd

As gunfire is exchanged by both sides, Huck picks up a rifle and begins firing back at the Alexandrians. Huck takes cover behind an overturned table as several of the deceased Saviors begin reanimating. Eventually, Huck is either gunned down or devoured by her zombified comrades.
