Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Virginia
[
]


At some point in the apocalypse, Adrian joined a community called the 
Alexandria Safe Zone
.



Season 6
[
]


"
JSS
"
[
]


Adrian is killed when 
the Wolves
 invade Alexandria and start ransacking houses and murdering residents. As 
Carol
 sneaks around the community in disguise, she witnesses a Wolf mutilating Adrian's body before using his blood to write a "W" on his forehead.



"
Now
"
[
]


The next day, Adrian's body is gathered by 
Tobin
 and 
Bruce
 and buried along with the other slain Alexandrians. His name is then added to the memorial wall by 
Betsy
 and two other 
women
.



Death
[
]


Killed By


Aaron
 
(Indirectly Caused)


Aaron leaves his backpack at Del Arno Foods, which causes the Wolves to locate and attack Alexandria.



The Wolves


As the Wolves make their way into Alexandria, Adrian is one of the many residents to be killed.
