Season 3
[

"
Made to Suffer
"
[
]


Warren assisted 
Merle
 and 
Martinez
 as 
Glenn
 and 
Maggie
 were set to be executed. However, the prisoners staged an ambush and Maggie killed Warren with a bone fragment, stabbing him in the throat.

Later, 
the Governor
 was informed of Warren's death from Merle, which led him to send the Woodbury Army to search for the intruders.



Death
[
]


Killed By


Maggie Greene


Warren is stabbed in the throat by Maggie, using a bone from the walker that Glenn had killed in the interrogation room. It is assumed that he was put down before reanimation.
