Post-Apocalypse
[

Season 5
[
]


"
Strangers
"
[
]


Albert is seen sitting down at the campfire, using his knife as a fork and eating a piece of 
Bob Stookey
's leg.



"
Four Walls and a Roof
"
[
]


Albert continues to eat Bob's leg, and as Bob starts to laugh hysterically Albert gets up with the others. As Bob says that he's tainted meat, Albert vomits up what he has ate, and asks what's going to happen to them now, with 
Gareth
 replying to calm down because they cooked his leg first before eating it. Later, Albert travels along with the other Hunters to the church, after 
Rick Grimes
 leads the stronger survivors out to finish off the remaining Terminants. Albert, in the church searches it with the others, and when he is about to open the door where the remaining group members are hiding, he is shot in the head by Rick.



Death
[
]


Killed By


Rick Grimes


As Albert and Mike are given the order to open the door to the room where the remaining members of Rick's group are hiding, he is shot in the head by Rick.
