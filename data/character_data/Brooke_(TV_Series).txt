Pre-Apocalypse
[
]


Location Unknown
[
]


Nothing is known about Brooke's life prior to or as the outbreak began.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Washington, D.C.
[
]


At some point in the apocalypse, Brooke somehow came into contact with a group named "the Saviors" which she subsequently joined upon where she was appointed as a worker for the Sanctuary.



Season 8
[
]


"
The Big Scary U
"
[
]


Brooke, along with 
José
, 
Potter
, and many other workers confronts the Savior lieutenants, demanding power, water, and other amenities. She demands the whereabouts of 
Negan
 whom she claims is supposed to protect the workers. When Negan starts whistling and makes his way toward the crowd, Brooke and the others quickly take a knee



"
Dead or Alive Or
"
[
]


Brooke is among the workers from the Sanctuary sent to 
Eugene
's 
outpost
 to help produce bullets for Negan's army.



"
Worth
"
[
]


Brooke is producing bullets with the other workers when she listens to Eugene's speech about meeting the minimum quota for 
Simon
. When a soot-covered Eugene returns after his brief absence, Brooke and the other Saviors are ordered to complete the entire bullet order in anticipation of Negan's final assault on the militia.



Season 9
[
]


"
Who Are You Now?
"
[
]


At some point following Rick's assumed death, the Sanctuary eventually falls as a community, with most of its residents integrating into Alexandria, Hilltop, and the Kingdom, though some chose not to join any of the communities. Since then, Brooke has not been seen among the communities after the Sanctuary fell, leaving her with an unknown fate.
