Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Washington, D.C.
[
]


At some point in the apocalypse, Leo somehow came into contact with a group named "the Saviors" which he subsequently joined.

Leo aided his group in terrorizing other survivors and communities around the area into subjugating to Negan's will, where their victims would be forced to give half their supplies in exchange for not being murdered.



Season 8
[
]


"
The King, the Widow, and Rick
"
[
]


While investigating a warehouse, Leo and 
Zia
 start talking about 
Negan
 when suddenly they alerted to the presence of 
Michonne
 and 
Rosita
 who are hiding behind some shelves. Instead of engaging in a gunfight, Leo is told by Zia to escape with the truck full of horn blares to 
Sanctuary
 in order to divert the herd outside the compound. Before he can go outside, Leo is confronted by Rosita who is using an RPG. After Leo calls Rosita's bluff, she uses it on him and he is blown up.



Death
[
]

Leo runs into Rosita who has an RPG Launcher pointed at him. He openly doubts her using the RPG on him to which she responds by firing and blowing him up.
