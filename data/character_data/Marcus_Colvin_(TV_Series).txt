Overview
[
]


Marcus is a well-spoken, confident, and seemingly high-status individual within the 
Commonwealth
. He is very sophisticated and respectful when talking with 
Yumiko
 and is willing to negotiate with her.



Pre-Apocalypse
[
]


Location Unknown
[
]


Nothing is known about Marcus' life prior to or as the outbreak began.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Ohio
[
]


At some point during the outbreak, Marcus joined a 
community
 in Ohio.



Season 11
[
]


"
Promises Broken
"
[
]


Marcus meets with 
Yumiko
 after she is approved for an upper class career by several recruiters. He informs her that 
Ms. Milton
 often takes a liking to fellow Harvard alumni, and that despite Yumiko's insistence on not staying in the Commonwealth permanently, he's sure her first encounter with Ms. Milton will convince her otherwise. When asked about the whereabouts of her companions, Marcus tells Yumiko they're clearing out 
buildings
 for future development as punishment for their crimes. Yumiko tells Marcus that if she's expected to provide legal services for Ms. Milton and her cabinet, that she'd like to arrange a direct meeting with her as soon as possible. Marcus states that such an arrangement would be difficult given 
Lance
's expectations of timing. When questioned of Lance's authority, Marcus admits Lance isn't his supervisor and ultimately agrees to arrange the meeting.



"
Faith
"
[
]


Marcus prosecuted Eugene Porter for the murder of Sebastian. He was successful and won the case as ruled by 
Judge George
.



"
Rest in Peace
"
[
]


With the city breached and many civilians killed as reported by 
Colonel Vickers
 and the screams heard throughout the invasion, Marcus' fate is left unknown. It is possible he was let into the Estates with the crowd of terrified citizens or was rescued later on elsewhere in the city, or was killed by the undead.
