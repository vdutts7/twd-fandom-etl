Pre-Apocalypse
[
]


Location Unknown
[
]


Almost nothing is known about Bill Jenkins' life prior to or as the outbreak began, except that he was a veteran and was confined to a wheelchair and lived in an apartment building with 
David Chambler
 and 
Miss Wilhelm
.



Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Season 4
[
]


"
Live Bait
"
[
]


After the outbreak began, Bill seemed to have attempted suicide by shooting himself in the face in his bathtub. However, Bill didn't succeed in destroying his brain so he reanimated. Bill was then found by 
Philip Blake
 many months later who put him down with a knife to the head.



Death
[
]


Killed By


Himself 
(Suicide, Alive)


Sometime during the outbreak, Bill shot himself in the face, but the bullet didn't make it to his brain, thus he reanimated.
