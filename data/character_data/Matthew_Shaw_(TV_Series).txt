Leah Shaw

Post-Apocalypse
[
]


Season 10
[
]


"
Find Me
"
[
]


While discussing events related to losing their loved ones that broke them, Leah tells Daryl she had an adopted son whose mother - who was like a sister to Leah - died giving birth to him. She goes on to explain that, one night, when her 
group
 was attacked by a large 
herd
, Matthew was bitten and that she didn't realize until they had run for miles and found an abandoned 
cabin
. Matthew later either succumbed to the infection, or was put down out of mercy by Leah before he could turn, and was in turn buried in the backyard by his grieving adoptive mother.
