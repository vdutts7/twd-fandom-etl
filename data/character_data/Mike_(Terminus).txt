Bob Stookey

"
Four Walls and a Roof
"
[
]


Mike continues to eat Bob's leg. 
Gareth
 mentions that 
Greg
 and Mike were "this close" from capturing that "
gray-haired bitch
" who killed his mother. As Bob starts to laugh hysterically, Mike gets up with the others. As Bob says that he's tainted meat, Mike spits and throws the food he is holding in anger, but is calmed by Gareth when he says that they cooked his leg before they ate it. Later, Mike travels along with the other Hunters to the church after 
Rick Grimes
 leads the stronger survivors out to finish of the remaining Terminants. Mike in the church searches it with the others, and when he is about to open the door where the remaining group members are hiding, he is shot in the head by Rick.

[
]


Killed By


Rick Grimes


As Mike and Albert are given the order to open the door to the room where the remaining members of Rick's group are hiding, he is shot in the head by Rick.
